AUTOMOTIVE PROGRESSIVE DIE PIECE PART

At Vito Technologies we offer solutions that deliver efficient, cost-effective, progressive dies to our customers.

Designed for use within a commercial truck door latch, this component is comprised of .120” cold rolled carbon steel.
