Value Added Services

In keeping with our more than 10-year commitment to superior, turnkey service, VITO offers a variety of value-added services.

VALUE ADDED SERVICES
CNC Machining – Our captive machining capability provides accurate, reliable components for our customers.
Wire EDM – This industry-first department provides high-precision cutting for metal components.
Metrology – These high-tech software-based inspection services ensure precise and accurate parts.
Prototyping – Rapid prototyping demonstrates part viability before production.
Low-volume & High volume Production Stamping – Stamping can supplement the production process and provide customer cost savings.Our production facility is fitted with progressive(line) die presses ranging from 60 to 200 tons to produce customer's simple to complex stamping needs.
VITO-managed Outside Processes & Services – Deburring, Screen printing, Fabrication (special), Heat Treating, A-Coat / E-Coat, Painting & Powder Coating, Metal finishing, Passivation, Plating (nickel/chrome, tin, zinc, zinc nickel), Salt spray, temperature, cycle, and other performance testing requirements, Specialty Packaging & Logistics
