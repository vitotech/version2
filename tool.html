Tool & Die Engineering

At VITO, In providing Tool and & Die solutions for its customers, VITO Tool & Die draws upon the vast experience of its knowledgeable team. our process begins with a partnership between our design and engineering team and the client. We continue to work with the customer every step of the way, from complete Simulation and Prototype to 3D Design and part production validation. With open communication, we obtain valuable feedback throughout our production process to ensure customized, precise tooling that meets all industry and customer standards while optimizing production processes. In this way the ultimate design takes into consideration all of our customer's function, quality requirements and cost saving. This ensures the manufacturability and repeatability of your components. This approach minimizes the risks associated with any new program launch and maximizes success.

Tools are designed using the latest 2-D and 3-D CAD/CAM technology; allowing VITO to share files with customers and reduce product development time. VITO offers complete prototype services.

VITO engineering services include:
* Die Simulation – Using leading-edge technology, our engineers perform simulations to validate tooling designs and part-material selections.
* 3D Modeling & CAD –We are able to design and test even the most intricate dies to verify their effectiveness for your manufacturing process.
* Die Design – Our design team includes engineers, designers and die makers with years of expertise in metal stamping tooling systems. We work closely with customers to develop innovative, cost-effective solutions to any tooling need.
